## Key Insights for KidCanvas TikTok Strategy

### Content Formats That Work:
1. **Day-in-the-life** - Show real parenting moments
2. **Before/After** - "What I thought parenting was vs reality"
3. **Tips/Hacks** - Quick parenting tips
4. **Relatable struggles** - "POV: You're a parent and..."
5. **Kid quotes** - Parents sharing funny/adorable kid quotes
6. **Memory making** - Capturing special moments (PERFECT for KidCanvas!)
